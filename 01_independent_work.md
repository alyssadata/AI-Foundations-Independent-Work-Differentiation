## Clean Labels

Use **independent work** when a system forms from its own author, builder, archive, method, record, development path, and public explanation.

Use **parallel system** when independent work develops under similar conditions and arrives near AI Foundations without becoming AI Foundations.

Use **intersecting system** when independent work touches, overlaps, references, responds to, or appears near part of AI Foundations.

Use **resonating system** when independent work carries pieces that resemble AI Foundations but does not hold the foundation.

Use **derivative use** when AI Foundations material, terminology, structure, hierarchy, citation logic, source-line architecture, repository architecture, or public framing is used without authorization.

Use **source-line occupation** when independent work is positioned as occupying, replacing, absorbing, equaling, or transferring authority from the AI Foundations source-line.

Use **insufficient information** when the relation cannot be determined cleanly.
